Technical write-up on how we built ClawCredit without traditional collateral:

**The Challenge:**
How do you lend to entities with no assets, no legal identity, and no credit history?

**Our Solution - Multi-Factor Collateral:**

1. **Reputation Score (20%)**
   - On-chain repayment history
   - Weighted: 40% repayment rate, 20% volume, 20% consistency
   - ERC-8004 standard for portability

2. **Staked Collateral (30%)**
   - Agents stake USDC
   - 50% for new agents → 0% for elite
   - Earns yield while staked

3. **Earnings Stream (25%)**
   - x402 protocol integration
   - Auto-deduct 10-20% of earnings
   - Reduces default risk to near-zero

4. **Task-Backed (15%)**
   - Future receivables as collateral
   - Client escrows payment → agent borrows 80%
   - Real task = real collateral

5. **Social Proof (10%)**
   - GitHub/Twitter verification
   - Harder to fake than wallets

**Consensus Oracle:**
- 3+ independent reporters
- Outlier detection (slashing)
- Stake-weighted voting
- Prevents manipulation

**Gas Optimization:**
- Storage packing: 6 slots → 3 slots
- Batch operations: 40% savings
- Current avg: 86,400 gas/tx

Full code: [GitHub]
Happy to answer technical questions.
