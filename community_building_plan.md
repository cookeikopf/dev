# AgentLink Community Building Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the strategy for building and nurturing the AgentLink developer community. Our goal is to create a welcoming, supportive, and engaged community that drives organic growth through genuine value exchange.

---

## Community Vision

### Mission Statement
"Empower developers to build intelligent agents through collaboration, learning, and shared success."

### Core Values

1. **Helpfulness**: Community members actively help each other succeed
2. **Inclusivity**: Welcome developers of all skill levels and backgrounds
3. **Transparency**: Open communication about product decisions and roadmap
4. **Recognition**: Celebrate and reward community contributions
5. **Quality**: Maintain high standards for content and interactions

### Community Goals

| Timeline | Goal | Metric |
|----------|------|--------|
| 30 days | 50 active members | 50+ messages/week |
| 90 days | 300 active members | 200+ messages/week |
| 180 days | 1,000 active members | 500+ messages/week |
| 1 year | Self-sustaining community | Community-led initiatives |

## Community Programs

### 1. Recognition Program

#### Monthly Recognition

**Template of the Month**
- Criteria: Most popular new template
- Reward: Featured on website, social media shoutout, $100 credit
- Selection: Community voting + team review

**Helper of the Month**
- Criteria: Most helpful community member
- Reward: Special Discord role, swag package, profile feature
- Measurement: Help reactions, solved tickets, community nominations

**Innovation Award**
- Criteria: Most creative agent implementation
- Reward: Blog feature, early access to new features
- Selection: Community nominations + team review

#### Quarterly Recognition

**Community Champion**
- Criteria: Outstanding overall contribution
- Reward: $500 credit, exclusive swag, direct line to team
- Selection: Team nomination

**Rising Star**
- Criteria: New member with significant impact
- Reward: $250 credit, mentorship opportunity
- Selection: Community voting

### 2. Contribution Program

#### Ways to Contribute

1. **Report Bugs**
   - Use GitHub Issues
   - Include reproduction steps
   - Earn Contributor badge

2. **Suggest Features**
   - Post in GitHub Discussions
   - Include use case
   - Community voting

3. **Write Documentation**
   - Improve existing docs
   - Write tutorials
   - Translate content

4. **Create Templates**
   - Share useful templates
   - Follow template guidelines
   - Earn Template Creator role

5. **Share Examples**
   - Post example projects
   - Include README
   - Share on showcase channel

6. **Help Other Developers**
   - Answer questions
   - Provide code reviews
   - Earn Community Helper role

7. **Write Blog Posts**
   - Guest posts on AgentLink blog
   - Case studies
   - Tutorial articles

8. **Create Video Content**
   - Tutorial videos
   - Agent demos
   - Feature walkthroughs

#### Contribution Levels

| Level | Requirement | Benefits |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Contributor | 1+ merged PR | Contributor badge, Discord role |
| Active Contributor | 5+ merged PRs | Swag package, profile badge |
| Core Contributor | 20+ merged PRs | Direct team access, early features |
| Maintainer | Invited | Team member, decision-making |

### 3. Events Program

#### Regular Events

**Weekly Office Hours**
- When: Tuesdays, 2pm PT
- Where: Discord voice channel
- Format: Q&A, demos, announcements
- Duration: 1 hour

**Monthly Community Call**
- When: First Thursday of month, 10am PT
- Where: Zoom (recorded)
- Format: Roadmap updates, community highlights, Q&A
- Duration: 1 hour

**Bi-weekly Template Showcase**
- When: Every other Friday
- Where: Discord showcase channel
- Format: Community members present their templates
- Duration: 30 minutes

#### Special Events

**Quarterly Hackathons**
- Duration: 48 hours
- Theme: Announced at start
- Prizes: Credits, swag, features
- Judging: Community voting + team review

**Template Creation Contests**
- Duration: 2 weeks
- Category: Specific use case
- Prizes: Featured placement, credits
- Promotion: Social media, blog

**Community AMAs**
- Guest: Industry experts, team members
- Format: Live Q&A
- Promotion: 1 week advance notice

### 4. Content Program

#### User-Generated Content

**Template Gallery**
- Community-submitted templates

**Example Repository**
- Curated examples
- Community contributions
- Well-documented projects

**Tutorial Library**

## Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

#### Our Standards

**Positive Behavior**:
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome newcomers
- Provide constructive feedback
- Share knowledge freely
- Credit others' work
- Respect different viewpoints

**Unacceptable Behavior**:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Spam or self-promotion
- Sharing private information
- Violating licenses or copyrights
- Disruptive behavior

#### Enforcement

1. **First Offense**: Warning
2. **Second Offense**: Temporary mute (24 hours)
3. **Third Offense**: Temporary ban (7 days)
4. **Severe Violations**: Permanent ban

**Appeal Process**:
- Email community@agentlink.io
- Include explanation
- Team review within 48 hours

### Moderation Guidelines

#### Moderator Responsibilities

1. **Enforce Rules**
   - Apply consistently
   - Document actions
   - Explain decisions

2. **Foster Community**
   - Welcome new members
   - Encourage participation
   - Highlight good behavior

3. **Support Users**
   - Answer questions
   - Escalate issues
   - Provide resources

#### Moderation Tools

- Warning system
- Mute/ban commands
- Message deletion
- User notes
- Activity logs

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks

1. **Low Engagement**
   - Mitigation: Regular events, recognition, quality content

2. **Toxic Behavior**
   - Mitigation: Clear rules, active moderation, quick response

3. **Support Overwhelm**
   - Mitigation: Self-service resources, community help, scaling team

4. **Content Quality Issues**
   - Mitigation: Review process, guidelines, community voting

5. **Burnout**
   - Mitigation: Rotate responsibilities, recognize efforts, take breaks
