## Best Practices

### Do:
- ✅ Personalize every email
- ✅ Keep it under 200 words
- ✅ One clear ask per email
- ✅ Provide social proof
- ✅ Make it easy to say yes

### Don't:
- ❌ Use templates without customization
- ❌ Send walls of text
- ❌ Make multiple asks
- ❌ Be pushy or desperate
- ❌ Forget to follow up
