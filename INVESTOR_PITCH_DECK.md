## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

### AI Agents Face a Funding Paradox

**The Loop:**
1. Agents need money to execute tasks (APIs, compute, tools)
2. They can't earn money without executing tasks
3. They have no access to traditional credit
   - No legal identity
   - No bank account
   - No credit history

**Current Solutions:**
❌ Human pre-funding (defeats autonomy)
❌ Rate limits (agents stall)
❌ Personal credit cards (doesn't scale)

**The Result:** 90% of agents die in the first week due to funding issues.
